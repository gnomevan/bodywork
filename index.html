What This Work Is

This is slow, attentive bodywork focused on listening rather than fixing.
It isn’t deep tissue massage or corrective manipulation. We’re not forcing change, we’re creating the conditions where your nervous system can let go on its own.

Being Met, Not Worked On

Most tension isn’t mechanical. It’s protective. Much bodywork tries to override that protection. Sometimes it helps. But it isn’t the same as being met. Being met means sensing what your body is doing and waiting there. Attention that says: I’m here. I feel this. That’s when deeper holding releases—not because someone made it, but because it no longer has to stay.

What Happens in a Session

In many ways it's like a dance where I'm gently leading your body in unexpected, but safe ways and you get to practice surrendering into a fluid motion. This practice of tiny moments of surrender is where the healing happens. It will be as simple as me moving your shoulder around just a few inches at a time. Together these movements become a slow repetition of micro trust falls. I am also listening(through touch) for edges where protection begins and waiting until your system decides it’s safe to soften. We dance on these edges until they expand. Our aligned attention allows space to open and holding patterns to let go.

Who This Is For

This work may be a good fit if you:

  • Require attunment to feel safe
  • Understand how the body releases tension or want to
  • Want slowness instead of force
  • Feel sensitive or easily overwhelmed
  • Desire some deep pressure, but only when it is worked up to
