Workshops

We all need more physical touch. I teach people how to listen through their hands — how to discover what feels good, how to ask without words, and how to offer presence through contact. Consensual attuned touch is both a massage skill and a basic human need.

Touch is a feedback loop, a beyond-verbal conversation that develops our somatic vocabulary. Participants learn to notice where protection begins and what it takes for the body to soften. People leave with a felt sense of what it means to be met, and tools to offer that experience to others.

The Workshop

Somatic Touch Workshop

A deeply relaxing, hands-on workshop where we practice attuned touch in pairs. You'll learn the dual roles of holder and held — discovering the edge between resistance and range of motion, between guarding and letting go.

We trade partners throughout so you can experience how different bodies carry tension and what it feels like to be met by different hands. The workshop moves from structured exercises into a deeper, non-verbal space where the body leads.

Come alone or bring a partner. Whether you want to give better massages or desperately need to receive one, this workshop offers both.

"At times this may feel like receiving the most attuned massage of your life."
