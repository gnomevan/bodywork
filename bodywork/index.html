What My Sessions Are Like

This modality is like a dance, but you're relaxed on the table. I gently lead parts of your body in safe but unexpected directions while you practice surrendering into fluid motion. Together these movements become a slow repetition of micro trust falls.

I'm listening through touch for edges where protection begins, waiting until your system decides it's safe to soften. We dance on these edges until they expand.

What people notice most is being met—really listened to. Afterward, something settles. The body stops holding itself together so tightly. One client described it as feeling herself fall into alignment.

Is This For You?

You'll thrive in this work if you...

  • Are sensitive, attuned, or identify as neurodivergent
  • Want real results and are willing to participate
  • Are curious about your holding patterns
  • Want some deep pressure, but only when approached gradually
  • Are drawn to yoga, meditation, or growth work

This isn't the right fit if you...

  • Want to fully check out while being worked on
  • Are expecting a traditional Swedish massage
  • Prefer to avoid looking at patterns or feelings
  • Want to be "fixed" rather than supported in your process

How this is different

  • Your attention is part of the process—at first
  • Once trust builds, you can relax into ease
  • We're retraining protective patterns, not forcing tissue
  • I work on joints, attachment points and mind-body connection

What to know

  • No lotion or oil
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Immediate results are common
  • No prerequisites—just a desire to change
  • Strong emotions often come up
