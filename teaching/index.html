Current Workshops

Conscious Attuned Touch

A deeply relaxing, hands-on workshop where we practice attuned touch in pairs. You'll learn the dual roles of holder and held, discovering the edge between resistance and range of motion, between guarding and letting go.

We trade partners throughout so you can experience how different bodies carry tension and what it feels like to be met by different hands. The workshop moves from structured exercises into a deeper, non-verbal space where the body leads.

"At times this may feel like receiving the most attuned massage of your life."

Come alone or bring a partner. Whether you want to give better massages or desperately need to receive one, this workshop offers both.

Dating the Party

A belonging practice for gatherings. Instead of clinging to people you know, hunting for romantic interest, performing a version of yourself, or retreating into your phone, you enter into relationship with the whole gathering. You stay open, let your body guide you, and get real with whoever is in front of you.

We start in the body, shaking off the defaults, getting out of your head. From there, three practices build on each other: getting honest with whoever is in front of you, feeling the energy of the room, and opening the circle so nobody stays on the edges. By the end, you'll have tools you can use at any gathering that foster connection and belonging.

Success looks like: you had a good time, you met some new people, you deepened existing relationships, you added to the vibe, and you felt like you belonged. Designed for festivals, retreats, and any gathering where people want to connect for real.
